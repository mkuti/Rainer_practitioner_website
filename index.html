Amatsu

A gentle Japanese therapy

Unlike many other therapies, Amatsu is a whole body treatment that works with the body's soft tissue (eg muscles, tendons, fascia, ligaments and organs) to help to re-balance other key mechanical and energetic structures (eg the spine,bones, joints, cranium and meridian circuits).
