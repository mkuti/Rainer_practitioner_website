Amatsu

Amatsu is an exceptional, full body treatment suitable for all ages and beneficial for a wide range of painful problems and ill-health.
Rooted in the ancient Japanese art as practised by Dr Hatsumi and developed by Dennis Bartram, Amatsu is soundly based on western scientific principles of anatomy, physiology, bio-tensegrity, and proprioception.

Amatsu Therapy uses a range of soft tissue work techniques to realign your physical structure, thus improving the efficiency of your muscles, fascia, tendons, and ligaments.
This gives the body space again so joints and the body's functional organs can work efficiently, whilst improving movement.

It looks at the interaction of five factors for health referred to as Godai (Go being the Japanese for five).
These five factors: Structure, Nutrition, Energy, Emotion and Environment are inter-dependent and can cause illness in any of the other areas.
